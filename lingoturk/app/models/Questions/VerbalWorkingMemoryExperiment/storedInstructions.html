Dear participant,

 

Thank you for participating in this experiment!

 

For this test, you will be presented with series of sentences. You will have to judge whether these sentences are acceptable or not. After judging the acceptability of 2 or more sentences, you will be asked to write down in the given slots the last word of the sentences, but not the last word of the first sentence! So if you have judged the acceptability of 2 sentences, you will only have to write down the final word of the second sentence. You will have to do this for 25 series of sentences. This test will take approximately 30 minutes.

 

If possible, write down the words in the correct order in which you saw them; so first the final word of sentence 2, then the final word of sentence 3, etc (but not the final word of the first sentence!). If you cannot remember all of the words in the order in which you saw them, please write down whatever words you can remember.

 

Please try to perform the acceptability task very accurately, and then perform as well as you can on the word memory task. Both tasks are important for this test, so please do not focus only on one of the two tasks.

 

To illustrate the test, consider this example of a set of sentences:

Please decide if the given sentence is acceptable or not.
The computer annoyed the scientist that was working very hard.

Is this sentence acceptable or unacceptable?(correct answer: acceptable, because the scientist can be described with the expression "that was working very hard" even if "who..." would be more typical)

Please decide if the given sentence is acceptable or not.
It was the book that the boy surprised.

Is this sentence acceptable or unacceptable? (correct answer: unacceptable, because boys don’t surprise books)

Please decide if the given sentence is acceptable or not.
The picture that the photographer took amazed the man..

Is this sentence acceptable or unacceptable? (correct answer: acceptable, because a picture can amaze a person)

Please answer the last word of each sentence you have seen before, except for the first sentence you have seen.

If you have any questions, please ask the experimenter now. If you are ready to start the test, please click ‘start’.
